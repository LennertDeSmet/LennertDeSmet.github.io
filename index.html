Hi

I am a second year PhD student at the DTAI research group of the KU Leuven under the supervision of professor Luc De Raedt. My research interests are mainly related to Neural-Symbolic AI (NeSY), more specifically located at the intersection between statistics, logic and optimisation. Currently, my research is focused on improving the scalability of probailistic NeSy methods through sampling and gradient estimation applied to hybrid (discrete + continuous) probabilistic logic.
